Originally written in 2019 · Republished December 17, 2025

I found this piece i wrote back in 2019:

The most important relationship is the one you have with yourself — and most people don't have a good relationship with themselves.

If you are like most people, then you better develop a better relationship with yourself. Because you can't break up with yourself like you do in other relationships. No matter what, you'll always be with yourself till the end.

The first step is to love yourself. By that I mean truly loving yourself through actions. You can't just say "I love myself" and do nothing. You have to back it up with actions. Actions over intentions.

There is a line I love from my favorite Batman movie:

"It's not who you are underneath, it's what you do that defines you."

Underneath you might think you love yourself, but if you don't back it up with self-loving actions, then you don't really love yourself.

Most people will say they love themselves and will act in self-destructive ways. If you are one of those people, then I'll give you this one piece of advice — through a book title by Kamal Ravikant: Love Yourself Like Your Life Depends On It.

Now go do that.
